ABOUT US

The Geological Institute of Ethiopia:

Was founded in 1968 as a department within the then Ministry of Mines, Water & Energy; Became autonomous in 1982 as Ethiopian Institute of Geological Surveys (EIGS), being accountable to the Ministry of Mines and Energy; Re-established as Geological Survey of Ethiopia  in 2000; As a result of recent reform (2022), it was renamed the Geological Institute of Ethiopia

As per Regulation no.501/2022, The GIE is mandated to undertake:

  1. Studies
  2. Research
  3. Consultancy services
  4. Trainings in the sector (in the field of geoscience) being accountable to the Ministry of Mines
Objective

Generating data on minerals and other geological resources through methods of efficient, effective, and Development demand focused geological study and research that enhance:

  • agricultural production
  • industrial inputs
  • infrastructure, and making it accessible to national development.

Improve the coverage, quality and accessibility of any mineral resource deposits, geothermal resources,  geological hazards and other geological data by strengthening domestic research capacity in the sector and conducting research in various geological methods.
